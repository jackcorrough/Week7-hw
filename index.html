About Jack:

I was born in Hinsdale, Illinois and lived in North Aurora, Illinois for the first couple years of my life. I then moved to La Grange, Illinois where I have lived for the last 20 years. Although I moved again after already living in La Grange, I was still in the city boundary. I attended Lyons Township High School and graduated in 2019 and have been studying at the University of Iowa ever since. I will graduate this spring with degrees in both finance and business analytics & information systems. Outside of class I like to play and watch sports.
